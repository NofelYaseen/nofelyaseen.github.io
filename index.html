CV (Jan 2023)

Nofel Yaseen

I am a Research Scientist at Meta, where I am part of Privacy Preserving Machine Learning (PPML) Ads Infrastructure organization. My work has been recongnized 2020 Facebook Fellowship in Networking and Connectivity and papers in NSDI, OSDI, SIGCOMM and Hotnets. Prior to Meta, I received my PhD student from the University of Pennsylvania (Upenn). At Upenn, my advisor was Dr. Vincent Liu. I also colloborated with Behnaz Arzani, Ryan Beckett and Minlan Yu. Prior to that I completed my Bachelor degree in Computer Science from the Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS), Pakistan, where I worked with Dr. Ihsan Ayyub Qazi, Dr. Zartash Uzmi and Dr. Fareed Zaffar. My research interests are in the broad areas of distributed systems and networking but include a number of related topics. My recent work aims to design a fine-grained network measurement tool and how we can use these measurements to understand the network traffic patterns.

Program committees: NSDI 2024, EuroSys 2022 Artifact Evaluation, CoNext 2022 Artifact Evaluation, Sigcomm 2022 Artifact Evaluation
